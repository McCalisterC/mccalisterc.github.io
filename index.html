About Me

Hello, I’m Corbin McCalister, an undergraduate Computer Science major specializing in Game Development at the University of Central Missouri. With a deep-rooted passion for game creation, I’m focused on building my expertise in game programming, while also nurturing my interests in 3D modeling and animation.

I bring experience in team collaboration and community engagement, having served as a Community Advisor at UCM, and I actively contribute to a dedicated game development team. Together, we’ve earned:

  • 1st place in UCM’s 2023 Game Jam
  • 1st place in UCM’s 2024 Game Jam
  • Best in Programming award at Texas A&M’s 2024 Chillennium Game Jam
